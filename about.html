My Story

I'm Oscar Zhou, a multi-disciplinary creative who finds inspiration at the intersection of technology and art. My journey began with music, where I discovered the power of expressing emotions through sound, particularly through the cello and piano.

This passion for creative expression naturally evolved into programming, where I found similar joy in crafting elegant solutions to complex problems. Today, I combine these passions to create digital experiences that are both functional and beautiful.
