How to Read the Ladder

Ranking System

Teams are ranked by competition points first. If teams are tied on points, goal difference and percentage are used as tie-breakers.

Column Meaning
P Games Played
W Wins
L Losses
GF Goals For — total goals scored by the team
GA Goals Against — total goals scored against the team
GD Goal Difference — GF minus GA
Pts Competition Points (2 for a win, 1 for a draw)
% Percentage — Goals For divided by Goals Against × 100

Example:

If a team scores 40 goals and concedes 30 goals:

  • GF = 40
  • GA = 30
  • GD = +10
  • % = 133.3
