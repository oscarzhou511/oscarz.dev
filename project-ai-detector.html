Improving the Accuracy & Efficiency of Detecting AI Text

A Modern, Private, On-Device Detector for AI-Generated Content

As AI-generated text becomes more prevalent, especially in academic and professional settings, it is crucial to reliably identify such content. This project aims to provide a secure, fast, and accurate detection tool that works entirely on-device, protecting privacy while exceeding 90% accuracy on public benchmarks.

  • All detection runs locally—no cloud, no leaks.
  • Accuracy benchmarked against RAID dataset and commercial detectors.
  • Interactive insights and visual explanations for every result.
